Welcome to BroomStack

About

BroomStack offers a more practical way to view curling stats! BroomStack's SeasonRanks feature allows you to search and filter through statistics for the last 13 curling seasons. Under the teams section, you can search teams and review their stats throughout the years, as well as check their all time win/loss records!

GitHub

BroomStack is 100% free and open source!

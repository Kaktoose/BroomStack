Stats Explained

  • PCT: GamesWon/TotalGames. Percentage of games won divided by 1000
  • HammerEfficiency: Percentage of ends converted with hammer (2 or more points)
  • ExtraEndHammer: Percentage of extra-end games won with hammer
  • ExtraEndSteal: Percentage of extra-end games won without hammer
  • Ends: Total amount of ends played in the season
  • PointsFor: Total points scored in the season
  • PointsAgainst: Total points conceded in the season
  • PointsFor/Game: Average points scored in each game
  • PointsAgainst/Game: Average points conceded in each game
  • AvgDiff: Average point differential
  • HammerPF/E: Average points scored in an end with hammer
  • HammerPA/E: Average points scored in an end without hammer
  • StealEfficiency: Percentage of stolen ends for a team
  • Force Efficiency: Percentage of forced ends without hammer
  • StealDefence: Percentage of ends where are steal is avoided
